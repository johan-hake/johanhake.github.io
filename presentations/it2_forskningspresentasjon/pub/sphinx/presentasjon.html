Numerical solution method¶

Numerical scheme:

\[u^{n+1} = \frac{1 - (1-\theta) a\Delta t}{1 + \theta a\Delta t}u^n, \quad n=0,1,\ldots,N-1\]

\(\theta=0\): Forward Euler, \(\theta=1\): Backward Euler, \(\theta=1/2\): Crank-Nicolson
